## Mission

Information Security Network, Inc. was a local nonprofit organization committed to promoting cybersecurity awareness through community outreach and education programs. Our mission was to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and tools needed to protect themselves in an increasingly digital world.

## Contributions

- **Website Development**: Developed and maintained the organization's website to enhance online presence and community outreach
- **Educational Content**: Created comprehensive content and resources promoting cybersecurity awareness within the community
- **Community Events**: Organized workshops and events engaging the public in security best practices and digital literacy
